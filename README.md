# Prosperity 2026

Repository for [IMC Prosperity](https://prosperity.imc.com/) trading competition strategies and tooling.

Prosperity 4 is a trading contest set in a simulated trading environment. This years contest was space themed and had 6151 teams in participation. I competed as a solo competitor.

## Phase 1

This round had two products to trade: Ash Coated Osmium and Intarian Pepper Root. Profiting from these products required using market making strategies, and the best strategies accurately calculate a fair mid value around which to trade. This included adjusting for situations like the orderbook emptying and separating noisy offers in the market from the ones pegged to the true value. I focused on creating a mid-price that was resilient to these market changes and noise by considering price history if the orderbook dried up or points of reference disappeared.


## Phase 1 — result

I finished the round with  34th of 6,151 teams on the overall leaderboard with 202,036 of the contests currency. 
This qualified me directly for phase 2 starting April 24th
